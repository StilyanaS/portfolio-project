Who am I?

Hello! I'm Stilyana, a frontend developer passionate about crafting elegant, maintainable, and scalable web applications.

While my current core expertise lies in Angular—a framework I've deeply specialized in over the past year, building performant, accessible, and user-centric applications—my frontend journey began much earlier. I bring years of hands-on experience with technologies like React, JavaScript (ES6+), PHP, Ruby, and MySQL, which gave me a solid foundation in both frontend and backend development.

This diverse technical background allows me to understand the full web stack, collaborate effectively with backend teams, and approach frontend challenges with a broader architectural perspective. My transition to Angular came naturally as I sought to focus on scalable solutions in enterprise-level projects.

I bring a strong foundation in TypeScript, RxJS, and modular component architecture, and I thrive when working on large-scale applications where clean code, maintainability, and team collaboration are key. My experience spans multiple industries, including consulting and enterprise services, where I’ve delivered robust frontend solutions and collaborated closely with cross-functional teams.

👩‍💻 What I Do

My day-to-day work involves building dynamic UIs, integrating REST APIs, writing unit and e2e tests, and optimizing applications for performance. I enjoy transforming complex business requirements into intuitive user interfaces and ensuring a high level of code quality throughout the development lifecycle.

I work fluently with tools like Angular CLI, SCSS, Git, Webpack, and RESTful services. I also enjoy working with agile teams using tools like Jira, Bitbucket, and Figma for cross-discipline collaboration. Whether I’m implementing lazy-loaded modules, handling reactive forms, or building reusable components, I always aim for scalability and readability.
